WHEREAS EISER Infrastructure Limited and Energia Solar Luxembourg S.a r.l.

(collectively “Petitioners”) obtained an arbitration award from the International Centre for

Settlement of Investment Disputes (“ICSID”) against Respondent, the Kingdom of Spain

(“Spain”), ICSID Case No. ARB/13/36 (the “Award”), certified on May 4, 2017;

WHEREAS Spain is required under the terms of the Award to pay Petitioners 128 million

Euro, together with interest from June 20, 2014 to May 4, 2017 at the rate of 2.07%,

compounded monthly, and interest from May 4, 2017 to the date of payment at the rate of 2.50%,

compounded monthly;

WHEREAS Petitioners commenced this proceeding by filing an Ex Parte Petition to

Recognize ICSID Arbitration Award Pursuant to 22 U.S.C. § 1650a (the “Petition”), dated May

19, 2017; and

WHEREAS Petitioners have complied with the requirements set forth in New York Civil
Practice Laws and Rules, §§ 5401 et seq., for recognition of judgments entitled to full faith and

credit;

IT IS ORDERED, ADJUDGED, and DECREED that Petitioners’ Petition is GRANTED;

and it is further ORDERED that:

a. The pecuniary obligations in the Award in favor of EISER Infrastructure Limited
and Energia Solar Luxembourg S.a r.1. and against Spain, shall be recognized and
entered as a judgment by the Clerk of this Court in the same manner and with the
same force and effect as if the Award were a final judgment of this Court, as

authorized by 22 U.S.C. § 1650a and Article 54 of the ICSID Convention; and

b. In accordance with the pecuniary obligations contained in the Award, Spain shall
pay to Petitioners the sum of 128 million Euro, together with interest from June
20, 2014 to May 4, 2017 at the rate of 2.07%, compounded monthly, and interest
from May 4, 2017 to the date of payment at the rate of 2.50%, compounded

monthly.

SO ORDERED.
